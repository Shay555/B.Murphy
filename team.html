CORPORATE

CORPORATE

  • Barry has been hosting corporate events as MC or After Dinner speaker for the last 20years.
  • He is one of the few Irish comedians who writes routines specific to the conference/event theme.
  • Barry provides an alternative and comical slant on conference presentations. He buries himself in the company and re-emerges as a visiting consultant who incorporates slides of sales figures/targets, advertising campaigns, company outings etc in his presentation.
  • His approach ensures that delegates embrace the ideas outlined in the conference and enjoy an extremely humorous and uplifting presentation.
  • Happy clients are too many to mention and include AIB, Bank of Ireland, PPI Radio awards, The Pendulum Summit, The Ireland Fund, Heineken Annual Conference, Diageo, Samsung, Porsche, Ryanair, Aer Lingus, Joe Duffy Motors, Boodles, The PFAI, The FAI, The Taxation Instititute, The Institute of Chartered Accountants, The ICADS, The Advertising Awards, Supervalu awards, Off Licence of the year, The Irish Independant Sports awards, The Marketing awards, The Outdoor advertising awards, The IFTAs, The Restaurant of the year Awards, The Institute of Directors...
