  • Barry Murphy is a 6 time IFTA nominated writer/comedian, probably best known as a third of the comedy trio Apres Match.
  • He has been described by the Irish Times as “The Godfather of Irish Comedy”
  • Co- founder of Ireland’s longest running comedy club, his TV creations include Irish Pictorial weekly, Soupy Norman, Couched and The End.
  • He has featured on Rory Bremner’s “International Satirists” and Armando Iannucci’s ‘Time Trumpet’ and the Stand Up Show for the BBC. He is currently writing a series of Audio Books and working on a new series of Apres Match of the Day for the RTE.
